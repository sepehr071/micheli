## About Patrizia Miceli

Patrizia Miceli has been running her own cosmetics studio since 2005. In 2011, her studio was recognized in the **Leistungswettbewerb des Deutschen Handwerks** (German Crafts Performance Competition) in North Rhine-Westphalia.

### Qualifications
- Certified Cosmetician (Ausbildung zur Kosmetikerin)
- Certified Foot Care Specialist (Ausbildung zur Fußpflegerin)

### Philosophy

*"Meine tägliche Motivation ist Ihnen ein gesundes und natürliches Aussehen zu verleihen."*
(My daily motivation is to give you a healthy and natural appearance.)

Patrizia believes that a positive inner attitude forms the foundation for a radiant exterior. Her approach prioritizes:
- **Holistic beauty** — combining inner wellbeing with outer appearance
- **Natural cosmetics** — respecting and supporting nature
- **Sustainability** — long-term results over quick fixes
- **Individuality** — every treatment is customized to the client
- **Innovation** — continuous professional development and modern techniques

She advocates that balanced nutrition, movement, relaxation, and self-care rituals together create a fulfilling lifestyle. Her motto: *"Hören Sie auf Ihr Herz"* (Listen to your heart).

## Services Overview

### Treatment Categories
- **Gesichtsbehandlungen** (Facial Treatments) — cleansing, anti-aging, hydration, specialized skin care
- **Körperbehandlungen** (Body Treatments) — body peeling, body wraps, contouring
- **Fußpflege** (Foot Care) — cosmetic pedicure, foot treatments
- **Maniküre** (Manicure) — nail care, hand treatments
- **Permanent Make-Up** — eyebrows, lips, eyeliner (detailed menu available as PDF)
- **Massagen** (Massages) — full-body massage, relaxation massage
- **Apparative Kosmetik** (Device-Based Cosmetics) — Forma radio frequency technology for skin tightening, collagen stimulation, and improved elasticity

### Methods & Brands
- **Brigitte Kettner Methode (MBK Cosmetics)** — natural cosmetics method focused on balance, protection, strength, and natural beauty
- **Forma Technology** — radio frequency technique for optimal skin contraction in deeper skin layers, stimulating new collagen formation
- **Apparative dynamische Kosmetik** — device-based dynamic cosmetics combined with natural cosmetics

## Atmosphere & Experience

Beauty Lounge aims to provide a relaxing retreat in a soothing atmosphere. Clients are offered coffee or tea during consultations and treatments. The studio emphasizes:
- A warm, welcoming environment
- Detailed personal consultation before every treatment
- Taking time for each client individually
- A holistic experience — not just a treatment, but a break from everyday life
